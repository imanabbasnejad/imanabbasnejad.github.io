Iman Abbasnejad

I am a Senior Machine Learning Research Fellow at The RMIT University Fugro neXt developing advanced technologies for Remotely Operated Underwater Vehicle (ROV). Before that I was working at MyFiziq developing machine learning and computer vision techniques for modelling 3D human body. Previously, I was a PhD student in a joint program between Queensland University of Technology (QUT) and The Robotics Institute (RI).
I worked closely with Prof. Simon Lucey and Prof. Sridha Sridharan.
